FINDER MapLibre mobile test checklist

1. One-finger drag
- The map moves immediately.
- A booth is not accidentally selected while dragging.

2. Short tap
- A booth is selected with one short tap.
- The selection panel opens without needing a second tap.

3. Pinch zoom
- Zoom in and out smoothly with two fingers.
- No booth is selected during pinch.
- The first booth tap immediately after pinch works.

4. Two-finger rotation
- Twist two fingers to rotate the map.
- Booth labels remain readable.

5. Two-finger pitch
- Move two fingers together vertically to change pitch.
- The map does not jump or disappear.

6. Route display
- Select a start point and destination.
- Route line and moving dots remain smooth while zooming/rotating.
- Clear route stops the animation immediately.

7. Special booths
- Diamond, crown, and event marker stay attached to their booths.
- Icons do not block booth selection.

8. Screen changes
- Rotate the phone between portrait and landscape.
- Full view, flat/3D, search, and selection still work.

9. Performance
- Try rapid zoom/rotate for 20-30 seconds.
- Check for lag, overheating, or browser reloads.
